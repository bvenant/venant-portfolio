My Journey

I’m a Full Stack Web Developer in Charlotte, NC. I love to take on new challenges and get out of my comfort zone – being a web developer has done just that!

How did I get here? Becoming a Full Stack Web Developer was a much-needed career change for me. I graduated from Appalachian State University with a BS in Business Management. I was laid off from my full-time job and decided to enter law enforcement. After 11.5 years in law enforcement, I did a complete 180 and switched my focus to Web Development – one of the best decisions I've made! I completed the Coding Boot Camp at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. Building code is my passion!

In my free time I enjoy hiking, traveling, and exploring new places with my wife and daughter!
